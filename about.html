About Us

Our Goal

We want to deliver as many game ideas as possible to help with your event planning. Providing activities for people to connect through makes for fun, lasting memories and happy smiles. We’re glad to be a part of the process! We hope you find games that will work for you and allow you to create happy memories and new connections.

Our Story

We know what it’s like to need game ideas. In college, we got together with friends frequently and learned tons of games that we still love and play to this day. We were able to connect with many people doing these games, and we’re looking forward to the many connections you will make by playing these too. We started learning so many that they had to be written down so we could remember how to play them all. We’ve found lots of joy playing group games that allowed us to meet and connect with many new people. We hope you find lots of joy playing these games!
